About Us

At The Far East, we bring the authentic flavors of UK-style Chinese cuisine and fresh sushi to the heart of Cyprus. Our chefs craft each dish with premium ingredients and traditional techniques, offering you a true taste of the Far East right here in Vrysoules.

Opening Hours:
Tuesday - Sunday: 17:00 - 22:00
Note: We are closed on Mondays.

Delivery Areas:
Vrysoules, Avgorou, Frenaros, and surrounding areas
